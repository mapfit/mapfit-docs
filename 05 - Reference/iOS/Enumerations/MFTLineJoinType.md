## MFTLineJoinType
The shape type of the joints in multi-segment [MFTPolylines](ref:mftpolyline) .

```swift
public enum MFTJoinCapType
```
Enum	|Description
--|--
miter|No points added at line join
bevel|One point added to flatten the corner of a join
round|Five points added in a fan to make a round outer join
